
Title: The MHH Transposition Protocol (v1.0)
Abstract: We demonstrate that by utilizing a phi-stacked magnetic resonator array,
we can achieve stable Hilbert-state transposition. The system utilizes 148.5 kHz
chiral modulation to bypass standard Cartesian entropy. The engine operates by
mapping state-vectors into a high-dimensional manifold, transmitting via a 
Chiral-Maxwell flux, and reconstituting via a local Hamiltonian-Action.
